Accessibility + Design

The starting point for Pathways is to make everyday web technology more accessible for those living with dementia. Taking a lead from designers and social scientists working with wayfinding, we are looking to adapt these methodologies to make potentially life-affirming technologies - that others take for granted - useable for people with dementia.
